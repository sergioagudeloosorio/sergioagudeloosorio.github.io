Subscription

Venturi offers three subscription plans. Each plan contains an evaluation and reporting guide to its corresponding modules. All three memberships can be cancelled at anytime.
  • Basic access is a plan designed for those who only choose one course. It includes one month access to the chosen course.
  • Extended access corresponds to an Aircraft Type Course and includes the privileges of a Basic plan, plus all the courses marked as Standard Courses for one month.
  • Unlimited access includes the privileges of the Extended Access plan, plus all the courses marked as Advanced Courses for a three month period.
