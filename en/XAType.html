Venturi courses lead to the highest quality of training in worldwide industry.

B-Learning Support for Type Rating Courses

Aviation training comprises multiple processes designed to keep and increase the safety index. The human factor represents a key component to guaranteeing safety. A creative training approach must be conducted in order to consider this fact and the accelerated growth rate of the industry.
Evolution of resources going hand in hand with advances in technology allows the aviation industry to improve such processes through providing personnel with comfortable, motivating tools to achieve complete focus during study sessions.
This, in turn, leads to a more effective use of training time, which ultimately lead to a long term reduction in training costs.
